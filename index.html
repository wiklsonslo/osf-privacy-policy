Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

The Opelika Songwriters Festival app ("the App") is operated by The Sound Wall ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your first name, last name, email address, and selected role (Attendee, Artist, or Volunteer). This information is stored on our servers and used solely for festival-related communication.

If you enable push notifications, we collect your device token to deliver festival announcements and schedule updates. Device tokens are stored securely on our servers and used exclusively for app notifications.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Payment or financial information
  • Third-party tracking or advertising data

Local Data

Your favorited performances and app preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Railway (backend hosting)
  • Cloudinary (image delivery)
  • Apple Push Notification service (notifications)

These services process data only as needed to deliver app functionality. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Guest Access

You may use the App without registering. Guest users do not provide any personal information.

Data Retention

Registration data is retained for the duration of the festival season and may be deleted upon request.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us using the information below.
